Outside the Burrow

At first you notice that none of the trees have snow on their branches, and, in fact, nothing appears snow-covered except the ground. Then you think "wait-a-minute... it's warm outside! This couldn't be snow! Hmmm.."

As you step out onto this mysterious ground surface, you notice its odd springy texture. It's kinda like walking on a feather mattress, but a bit bouncier. You reach down to feel the surface, but your hand seems to pass right through it. You notice your feet are slightly hidden in a light fog, which is otherwise invisible against the white ground.]
